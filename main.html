Welcome to my-xmpp!

my-xmpp is a service that lets you shorten URLs faster, by sending a chat message.

To start using my-xmpp:

  1. Sign into Google Talk or any other chat client that supports XMPP. IM Clients
  2. Send a chat invitation to my-xmpp@appspot.com
  3. Send URLs to the contact in your contact list

Contact: @cyberfart
